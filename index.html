auspex[ai]

A volunteer compute network for AI safety research.

AGPL-3.0 · donation-and-recognition · no crypto-economy

Every accepted contribution earns a signed receipt — recorded in the public transparency log, verifiable forever. Receipts accrue into your trust on the network, unlocking vouching, approver eligibility, and access to higher-tier work. For researchers, they’re the DOI of volunteer compute — citable in papers, durable as attribution.
